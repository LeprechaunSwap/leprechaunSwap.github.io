LeprechaunSwap Whitepaper

1. Introduction

LeprechaunSwap is a next-generation decentralised exchange (DEX) built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) that aims to provide users with a unique and innovative trading experience. In the rapidly evolving DeFi landscape, LeprechaunSwap addresses the need for a user-friendly, secure, and community-driven platform that focuses on exclusive token pairs, innovative liquidity mining incentives, and a robust governance model. Our target audience includes both experienced and novice crypto traders seeking exposure to promising projects in their early stages.
